Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving progress through groundbreaking ideas. Empowering Bangladesh with cutting-edge technology and transformative solutions. Shaping the future with creativity and vision

Sustainability

Building a greener tomorrow with eco-conscious development. Committed to protecting the environment for future generations. Fostering growth with sustainability at the core.

Infrastructure

Redefining connectivity with modern infrastructure. Empowering communities through smart and inclusive development. Laying the foundation for a prosperous Bangladesh.

Empowerment

Championing growth through inclusion and opportunity. Uplifting communities with education, skills, and resources. Empowering every individual to build a better tomorrow.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a nation built on innovation, sustainability, and empowerment. It’s a future of zero poverty, zero unemployment, and zero carbon emissions. Rooted in resilience and creativity, it leverages technology to uplift communities, bridges rural-urban gaps, and fosters inclusive growth. Sustainable infrastructure connects people and opportunities, while education and empowerment unlock individual potential. Together, we can create a prosperous, equitable, and eco-conscious Bangladesh a beacon of hope for the world

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
